ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -vf "crop=w:h:x:y" output.mp4
eg: ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -vf "crop=430:859:750:130" -c:a copy out.mp4 (for mobile webview screen recording)
join afte cut : ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -vf "crop=2450:1080:120:0" output.mp4

Where the options are as follows:

use "-vf" or -"filter:v" - depending on your version of ffmpeg/avconv
vf indicates the usage of a video filter.
crop is the name of the filter.
w:h is the width and height of the output video.
x:y is the coordinate where the video will be cropped.
